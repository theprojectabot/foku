    The Story Begins

    The old Master is betrayed by league of Shadow Monks as they steal the artifact known as the Great Fork. He sends his fellow students, a forksman and a wizard, to retrieve the Fork before Shadow Monks use it to open a portal into the Void, but will they succeed? Play and find out.

    Controls

    Movement
    A, D or arrow keys
    Jumping
    Space key
    Attack
    Left or Right Control key

    • Jump to avoid being hit
    • Deliver multiple quick blows to kill enemies before they regenerate
    • Large enemies can only be killed by striking their unprotected heads
    • If you perform few hits without getting hit yourself, your fellow assistant will deploy one of her spells automatically
    • Protective shield will not let enemies get inside, but it won't protect you against their weapons
